About Me:

I'm a GTM leader, currently leading revenue at Vero Technologies. Previously I helped build some great teams at ACV Auctions. I love building. I'm a co-founder of Refraction, an automotive SaaS company and Good Neighbor Fund, a micro-grant program supporting entrepreneurs.

I'm a husband and father of two amazing kiddos, Levi and Tinsley. When I'm not working, I'm usually tinkering with a new micro-project, pretending to be a photographer, or over-engineering my longevity protocol.
