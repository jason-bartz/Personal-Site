Jason's Blurbs

About Me:

I'm a GTM leader, currently leading partnerships at Vero Technologies. Previously I helped build some great teams at ACV Auctions. I love building. I'm a co-founder of Refraction, an automotive SaaS company and Good Neighbor Fund, a grant program supporting early-stage entrepreneurs.


I'm a husband, and father of two amazing kiddos, Levi and Tinsley. When I'm not working, I'm usually tinkering with a new project, taking photos, or over-engineering my longevity protocol.

Jason's Projects
Co-Founder, Refraction
I co-founded Refraction, an automotive SaaS platform built to future-proof dealerships. Think Shopify for car dealers. Together with my co-founder, we raised venture funding and brought our products to market. Additionally, I developed GhostLot, a standalone product within the Refraction ecosystem.
www.shoprefraction.com | www.ghostlot.com
Co-Founder, Good Neighbor Fund
I co-founded Good Neighbor Fund, a micro-grant program supporting ideation-stage founders. To date, we've awarded over $30k in micro-grants. I've led fundraising, built the brand identity and content strategy, grown our social presence, and developed the GNF platform.
www.neighborhoods.space
Creator, Your Life Weekly
I created Your Life Weekly, a free iOS app that visualizes your entire lifespan as a grid of ~4,000 weeks. Inspired by stoic philosophy, it makes time tangible through life visualization, weekly reflections, and personalized year and decade summaries.
www.yourlifeweekly.com
Creator, Good Vibes Games
I founded Good Vibes Games, my solo game development studio. Creating games that I would actually want to play.
www.goodvibesgames.com
Tandem Daily Games: Daily word puzzles, crosswords, and movie games. Available on iOS and web. www.tandemdaily.com
Daily Alchemy: A daily element crafting puzzle game. Combine water, fire, earth, and air to discover new elements and reach the target within a set number of moves. www.dailyalchemy.fun
Who I'd Like To Meet
